Top of Tower
You stand at the very pinnacle of the western tower. You can see for miles and miles in every
direction. The wind whips crazily through your hair.
You can see a ball and a stick here.

>GET STICK
Taken.

>SET STICK TO 5
You turn the hidden dial on the stick to 5.

>LIFT BALL
You lift the ball.

>ATTACH BALL TO STICK
You fasten the ball to the end of the stick.

>X BALL
The ball is a round plastic ball.

>DETACH BALL FROM STICK
You unfasten the ball from the stick.

>QUIT
